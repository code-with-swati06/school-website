About IT Club

The T.A.P.S IT Club is a place where students come together to explore the world of technology. Whether you love coding, designing, or just want to learn something new- you are welcome here!

We hold regular workshops, competitions, and fun activities throughout the year. Our goal is to make technology easy and exciting for every student in our school.

From building websites to learning about Web Development, our club covers it all. Join us and start your journey in tech today!

🌿Welcome to Eco Club

Learn. Act. Protect. Build a greener future with the Eco Club at T.A.P.S School, Meerut.

About Eco Club

The T.A.P.S Eco Club is a place where students come together to care for the environment and make a positive difference. Whether you love nature, enjoy planting trees, or simply want to help the planet — you are welcome here!

We organize regular clean-up drives, awareness campaigns, and eco-friendly activities throughout the year. Our goal is to inspire every student to protect and preserve the environment in simple, meaningful ways.

From learning about sustainability to practicing green habits, our club encourages responsible living. Join us and be a part of creating a cleaner, greener future!

What We Do

🌱

Tree Plantation

Students actively participate in planting trees and nurturing them, helping to create a greener and healthier environment.


🧹

Clean-Up Drives

We organize cleanliness campaigns in and around the school to promote hygiene, responsibility, and environmental awareness.


♻️

Recycling & Waste Management

Learn the importance of reducing waste, recycling materials, and adopting eco-friendly habits in daily life.


📢

Awareness Campaigns

Students spread awareness about environmental issues through posters, rallies, and presentations.


💧

Water Conservation

Activities and projects that teach students how to save water and use it wisely for a sustainable future.


🔋

Energy Conservation

Promoting smart use of electricity and encouraging energy-saving habits in school and at home.


🌍

Environmental Projects

Hands-on projects like composting, gardening, and eco-models to understand nature better.


🎨

Eco-Friendly Crafts

Create useful and creative items from waste materials, turning trash into something valuable.

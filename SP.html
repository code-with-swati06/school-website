About Sports Club

The T.A.P.S Sports Club is a place where students come together to compete, grow, and push their limits through the power of sport. Whether you love cricket, football, athletics, or just want to stay active and have fun — you are welcome here!

We hold regular practice sessions, inter-house competitions, and tournaments throughout the year. Our goal is to build discipline, teamwork, sportsmanship, and physical fitness in every student at our school.

From the cricket pitch to the athletics track, our club covers it all. Join us and start your championship journey today!

What We Do

🏏

Cricket

Train with proper batting, bowling, and fielding techniques. Students represent the school in inter-school tournaments and local competitions.


⚽

Football

Learn teamwork, tactics, and skills on the field. Students participate in matches and tournaments throughout the academic year.


🏃

Athletics and Track Events

From sprints to long jump, students build speed, strength, and endurance through structured training and competitive events.


🏆

Tournaments and Championship

Members compete in inter-house, inter-school, and district-level competitions, representing T.A.P.S with pride and passion.

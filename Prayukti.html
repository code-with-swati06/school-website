Prayukti Event Held At School

About Prayukti

Prayukti is an annual technology and robotics exhibition held at T.A.P.S, where students get the opportunity to showcase their creativity and technical skills. From building robots to presenting innovative science projects, Prayukti is a platform where young minds come together to explore the world of technology. Students work tirelessly for weeks, designing and developing their projects, making it one of the most anticipated events of the school year. It is not just an exhibition — it is a celebration of curiosity, innovation, and the spirit of learning.

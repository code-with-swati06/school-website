Sports Winners



Introducing Ujjaval Pradhan

Ujjaval Pradhan, a dedicated and talented student from Class 10th Akash, has made a remarkable mark in the field of football as a skilled goalkeeper. With strong determination and consistent hard work, he has earned the opportunity to participate in the Indian League, popularly known as the I-League U-15, which highlights his growing presence in competitive football. His abilities on the field have not only brought him recognition at the league level but have also helped him achieve the honor of being both a national-level and state-level football player. Ujjaval’s journey reflects his passion for the sport, his discipline, and his commitment to improving his skills, making him an inspiring example for other young athletes who aspire to achieve success in football.



Introducing Maan Yadav

Maan Yadav, a talented student of Class 10th, is a passionate and dedicated football player who plays as a midfielder. Known for his excellent control over the game and strong presence on the field, he has already achieved remarkable success at a young age. He has proudly participated in the prestigious I-League U-15, which showcases some of the best young football talents in the country. Along with this, Maan has earned recognition as both a national-level and state-level football player, highlighting his consistent performance and hard work. His journey reflects determination, discipline, and a deep love for the sport, making him an inspiring example for other young athletes who aspire to achieve success in football.



Introducing Aryan Singh

Aryan Singh, a dedicated student of Class 12th Commerce, is a talented and hardworking football player who plays in the forward/striker position. Known for his speed, sharp attacking skills, and goal-scoring ability, he has made a strong mark in the field of football at a young age. Aryan has proudly represented himself as both a national-level and state-level player, which reflects his consistent performance and commitment to the sport. In addition to this, he has also participated in the Delhi Youth League, where he showcased his skills against some of the best young players. His achievements highlight his determination, discipline, and passion for football, making him a role model for aspiring athletes.

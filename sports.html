Sports Day Held at School

Sports Day is one of the most energetic and exciting events of the year at T.A.P.S, where students step onto the field to compete, perform and celebrate the spirit of sportsmanship. From thrilling track and field races to team competitions, the day is packed with action and enthusiasm. Students train hard in the weeks leading up to the event, giving their best on the ground while their classmates cheer them on. It is not just about winning — it is about teamwork, determination and the joy of giving your all. Sports Day reminds every student that athletics and physical fitness are just as important as academics.

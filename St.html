Stem Exibition Held At School

Stem Exibition held at School

STEM Exhibition is an annual event at T.A.P.S where students bring science to life through working models, experiments and innovative projects. From physics demonstrations to chemistry experiments, the exhibition covers a wide range of scientific concepts explored and presented by students themselves. It is a platform that encourages curiosity, critical thinking and hands-on learning beyond the classroom. Every project tells a story of hours spent researching, building and discovering — making the STEM Exhibition one of the most inspiring events of the school year.

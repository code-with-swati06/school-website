About Us


The Avenue Public School (T.A.P.S) is a place where learning goes beyond textbooks and classrooms. Established with the vision of nurturing young minds, T.A.P.S provides a balanced education that combines academic excellence with moral values and creativity.


Our mission is to create a caring and stimulating environment where students are encouraged to explore their potential and become confident, responsible, and compassionate individuals. We believe that every child is unique and deserves the best opportunity to grow intellectually, emotionally, and socially.


With well-qualified teachers, modern facilities, and a strong focus on holistic development, T.A.P.S continues to uphold its commitment to excellence in education and character building.


Beyond academics, we emphasize co-curricular and extracurricular activities that help students develop leadership skills, teamwork, creativity, and discipline. From sports and cultural events to community service and innovation programs, we ensure that every child gets the chance to shine in their own way.

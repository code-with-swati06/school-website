About GK Club

The T.A.P.S GK Club is a place where curious minds come together to explore, discover, and learn about the world around them. Whether you love current affairs, history, science facts, or just enjoy knowing a little bit about everything — you are welcome here!

We hold regular quiz sessions, discussions, and knowledge competitions throughout the year. Our goal is to broaden the horizons of every student and make learning about the world fun, engaging, and inspiring.

From world history to the latest happenings across the globe, our club covers it all. Join us and feed your curiosity today!

What We Do

🌍

Current Affairs

Stay updated with what's happening around the world. Students discuss national and international news, events, and developments on a regular basis.


📚

History and Geography

Dive deep into the stories of civilizations, world leaders, great events, and the geography of our incredible planet.


🧠

Quiz Competitions

Students participate in exciting inter-house and inter-school quiz contests, sharpening their memory, thinking speed, and general awareness.


🏛️

Civics and Constitution

Learn about India's government, democracy, fundamental rights, and the Constitution in an engaging and interactive way.

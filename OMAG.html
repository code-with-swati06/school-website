Our Mission And Goal




To develop our students into responsible, harmonious and integrated individuals through ever evolving scholastic and co-scholastic activities. Our school offers many activities to create a positive learning environment and develop sense of competitive spirit, diligence, creativity and team spirit. The children are taken out frequently on educational and industrial sites for their direct exposure.

Getting proper education is very necessary to get success and a happy life just like food is necessary for healthy body. Education develops personality of the person, provides physical and mental standard and transforms person's living status.

THE AVENUE PUBLIC SCHOOL (TAPS) is a co-educational English medium school, that provides equal opportunities to both girls & boys.

TAPS prepares children, for leadership with roots in our culture and wings for the future. This is a modern educational institution based on traditional Indian system of learning and teaching.

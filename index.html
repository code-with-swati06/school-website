Introducing Rudrakshi Gupta

Rudrakshi Gupta, a student of Class 7th Prithvi, has brought immense pride to her school and city with her remarkable achievement. She wrote a touching and inspiring poem titled “The Lion of Kargil”, dedicated to the brave soldier Captain Vikram Batra. Through her simple and heartfelt words, Rudrakshi beautifully expressed the courage, sacrifice, and patriotism of a true hero. Her poem highlights how Captain Batra fearlessly fought for the nation and became a symbol of bravery and determination. She showed great understanding in portraying that real heroes never truly leave us, as their legacy continues to inspire generations. Rudrakshi’s achievement became even more special as she was selected among the Top 100 winners of Veer Gatha 5.0, a prestigious national-level competition. She was invited to receive her award and medals, which is a proud and memorable moment. What makes her accomplishment truly exceptional is that she is the only student from Meerut to be selected and invited to the President’s House (Rashtrapati Bhavan) to receive this honor. This is a rare and prestigious opportunity, and it highlights her talent, dedication, and hard work at such a young age. Rudrakshi Gupta has not only made her school proud but has also brought honor to her entire city. Her success is an inspiration for all students to dream big, work hard, and express their thoughts with confidence and creativity. We heartily congratulate Rudrakshi Gupta on this outstanding achievement and wish her many more successes in the future.

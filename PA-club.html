💃Welcome to Performing Art Club

Express. Perform. Inspire. Let your talent take the stage at T.A.P.S Performing Arts Club, Meerut.

About Performing Art Club

The T.A.P.S Performing Arts Club is a place where students come together to express themselves through the magic of performance. Whether you love dancing, singing, acting, or just want to discover a hidden talent- you are welcome here!

We hold regular rehearsals, stage performances, and cultural events throughout the year. Our goal is to build confidence, creativity, and teamwork in every student through the joy of the arts.

From school plays to annual day celebrations, our club brings stories to life on stage. Join us and let your talent shine!

What We Do

🎭

Threatre And Drama

Explore the art of storytelling through acting, scripts, and stage performances. Students develop confidence, expression, and public speaking skills.


🎤

Singing And Vocal Training

From classical to contemporary, students learn vocal techniques, harmonies, and perform in solo and group events.


💃

Dance

Covering classical, folk, and modern dance forms, students learn choreography and perform at school events and competitions.


🎬

Stage Production

Students get hands-on experience with set design, lighting, sound, and direction- learning what goes on behind the curtain.

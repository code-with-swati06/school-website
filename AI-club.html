About AI Club

The T.A.P.S AI Club is a place where students come together to explore the exciting world of Artificial Intelligence and emerging technologies. Whether you're passionate about building smart systems, training machine learning models, or creating real-world AI-powered projects- you are welcome here!

We hold regular workshops, competitions, and hands-on project sessions throughout the year. Our goal is to make Artificial Intelligence easy, accessible, and exciting for every student in our school.

From building intelligent chatbots to working with robotics and automation, our club covers it all. Join us and start your journey into the future of technology today!

What We Do

💻

Coding

Learn Python, HTML, CSS and more in fun hands-on sessions.


🤖

Robotics

Design, build, and program real robots. Students learn how machines interact with the physical world through sensors, motors, and intelligent control systems.


🧠

Artificial Intelligence and Machine learning

Understand how AI thinks, learns, and makes decisions. Students explore machine learning concepts and build their own basic AI models.


🌐

Web Development

Build real websites and web applications from scratch. Students learn front-end and back-end development as part of creating complete, working projects.
